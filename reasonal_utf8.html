FAQ

Frequentlyaskedquestions

Who is Reasonal for?

Reasonal is for professionals, students, lifelong learners, and anyone who wants to think more clearly and protect themselves from disinformation and manipulation. If you're tired of clickbait headlines, rage-bait arguments, questionable "studies" shared on social media, or conversations you later regretted wasting your time on, this app is for you.

What can I learn on Reasonal?

Reasonal teaches critical thinking, logical reasoning, data literacy, and media literacy through research-backed, non-linear lessons written by human experts.
You'll learn to spot logical fallacies, recognize cognitive biases, interpret statistics and research studies, and identify misinformation and manipulation tactics. Every skill applies to real life: news, work, money, decisions.

What are non-linear lessons?

Unlike traditional courses, Reasonal lessons adapt to you. Each lesson is built around real research and real-world examples, not hypothetical textbook scenarios. You choose what to explore, and the content changes based on your choices. If you already understand a concept, the lesson moves forward. If you want to go deeper, it lets you.

Is the content AI-generated?

No. Every lesson is written by human experts using verified sources. No AI generation, no hallucinations, no unverified claims or invented studies.

How is Reasonal different from other learning apps?

Exclusive content. Human experts research, fact-check, and write every lesson.
Research-backed lessons. Every lesson cites actual studies with names, dates, and data you can verify. You can be sure you are not learning AI slop.
Adaptive learning paths. The lesson changes based on your responses. Your path through a lesson won't be identical to someone else's.

How much does Reasonal cost?

Pricing varies by region. Reasonal offers monthly and annual plans, with the annual plan saving around 50%.
If you like Reasonal but can't currently afford a subscription, email us at hello@reasonal.co. We're happy to work something out.

Do you offer free trials?

Yes. Every new user gets a free trial with full access. No ads, no limits. We'll send a reminder before your trial ends.

How do I get a refund?

All Reasonal subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. Apple handles all purchase information and does not share billing details with developers, which means we cannot issue refunds directly. To request a refund, visit Apple's Report a Problem page, sign in with your Apple ID, select "Request a refund," and choose your Reasonal subscription. Refunds are at Apple's discretion. See Apple's official support page for details.
